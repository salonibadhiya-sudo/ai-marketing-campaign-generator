## ✏️ Customise for Your Brand

All campaign content lives inside the `<script>` block in `index.html`. To adapt it:

1. **Update the `days` array** — swap in your own topics, hooks, captions, and CTAs for each day
2. **Update the `viral` array** — replace ideas with ones relevant to your specific niche
3. **Update the `tips` array** — add your own learnings over time
4. **Swap brand mentions** — search for `#YourBrand` and replace with your actual Instagram handle
5. **Update the hero tags** — change platform, goal, audience, and location at the top
