01 · Overview

🎯 Campaign Strategy

The angle

"Real Girl, Real Style" — aspirational but relatable

Position your brand as the go-to fashion destination for modern Indian women in Indore & beyond — not just selling clothes, but selling an identity. The hook: content that feels like a best friend's wardrobe recommendation, not a billboard. Every post shows a real woman, a real occasion (college, mall, date night, Diwali prep), and exactly how to style it. Local relevance = massive relatability.

🎯 Core Message
You don't need a big budget to look stunning. Our pieces make every girl the main character — from Sarafa Bazaar to Sayaji Hotel brunch.
📌 Unique Hook
"Outfit of the day" storytelling tied to real Indore moments — college fests, chai meetups, Navratri nights. Hyper-local = hyper-shareable.

🎬 Ad Creative Concept
A 15-sec Reel showing 3 outfits styled 3 ways each — "one piece, three vibes." Hook text overlay: "POV: Your wardrobe finally understands you." End with a price reveal + Shop Now CTA. Use trending audio. Shoot in natural light — no studio needed.
👥 Audience Targeting
Location: Indore + Bhopal + Jabalpur. Interests: Myntra, Nykaa Fashion, Zara, Shein, trending fashion, online shopping. Behavior: engaged shoppers. Upload your existing customer list to build a Lookalike Audience.
📊 Expected Outcome
At ₹300–500/day: 8,000–15,000 impressions daily, 150–300 profile visits, 20–50 DM inquiries or link clicks per week. Optimize after Day 4 — pause underperforming creatives.
💡 Pro Tip
Run 2–3 ad variations simultaneously with different hooks but the same product. Let the algorithm pick the winner after 4 days, then put 80% of your budget on the best performer.

05 · Community & Growth

🤝 Growth Strategy

🌟 Influencer Strategy
Target nano (2K–10K) and micro (10K–100K) influencers in Indore, Bhopal, and Nagpur. DM 15–20 per week. Offer a free outfit (gifting) in exchange for one Reel + 2 Stories. Track ROI via unique promo codes per influencer.
🤝 Collaboration Ideas
Partner with local makeup artists, cafes (style shoots at their location = mutual exposure), wedding planners, and fitness coaches. Co-create content: "What I wore to my first date at 36 Galata" drives both audiences.
💬 Community Engagement
Reply to every comment in the first 30 minutes (boosts algorithm reach). Run weekly "Style your pick" polls in Stories. Create a customer hashtag and repost UGC. DM every new follower with a welcome discount.
🎊 Seasonal Moments
Indore-specific pegs: Navratri looks, Diwali party outfits, college farewell season (Feb–March), monsoon styling. These moments drive urgency and local authenticity — lean into them hard.
